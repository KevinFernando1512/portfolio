Duolingo is a popular, free language-learning platform available on both desktop and mobile devices.
It utilizes gamified lessons, short, daily exercises, and spaced repetition to make learning engaging and effective.
Duolingo offers courses in over 40 languages, including more niche options like Welsh, Irish, and even constructed languages like Klingon.
The platform also offers courses in math and music.
